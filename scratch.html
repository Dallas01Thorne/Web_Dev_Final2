Dallas Cowboys – One of my Biggest Passions

I have been a Cowboys fan literally since I was born. The first thing I ever wore was a little Cowboys onesie. My entire family are all die-hard Dallas Cowboys fans.

Fun Facts

Below is a list of fun facts related to my Cowboys fandom:

  1. I have an autographed jersey from legendary Cowboys receiver Dez Bryant.
    • In 5th grade my dad took me to a game in Dallas where I had the opportunity to be on the field before the game and Dez signed my jersey.
    • It is a memory that will stay with me forever
  2. I am literally named after the team. My dad's fandom is the reason I was named Dallas.
  3. I have had the opportunity to go to 3 Cowboys games and went on the field twice.
